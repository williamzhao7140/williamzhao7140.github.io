Subscription Help

Dove Plus subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID and the App Store.

To manage or cancel your subscription:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone
  2. Tap your Apple ID
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Select Dove
  5. Manage or cancel your subscription

Refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple. If you need a refund, please request it through Apple’s official purchase support process.
